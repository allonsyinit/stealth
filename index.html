About Us

Allonsy is creating technology to power the value-based revolution in higher education.


We started from a simple premise - what if universities focused on student success in the same way hospitals focus on long-term patient outcomes? Our team is developing advanced data and operating software to enable colleges and universities of all sizes to build a more effective learning environment. With Allonsy, universities will engage students and empower graduates to better outcomes.
