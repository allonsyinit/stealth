About Us

Allonsy is creating technology to power the value-based revolution in higher education.

We started with a simple premise: What if the digital strategies revolutionizing value-based healthcare could be transported to higher education? What if existing data could be used to increase efficiency, demonstrate value, and improve student outcomes?

Our team is developing advanced analytics and operating software for higher education institutions of all sizes. With Allonsy, colleges and universities will gain improved visibility into the factors that drive institutional performance and empower their students to achieve more - both on-campus and after graduation.
